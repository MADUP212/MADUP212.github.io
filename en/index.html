Marc-Antoine S. Dupuis

Master's student in Political Science at Universite Laval, student researcher at the Centre for Public Policy Analysis, and reservist working in logistics within the Canadian Armed Forces.

As both a servicemember and a student researcher, I am interested in Canadian and Quebec public policy, as well as social and economic issues. My current research focuses on Canadian census data and social stratification.
