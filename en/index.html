Welcome!

Marc-Antoine Dupuis holds a B.A. in Political Science from UQAM (2025) and is a Master’s student in Political Science at Université Laval (2025–2027). He works on Quebec and Canadian public policy at the Centre for Public Policy Analysis since January 2026.
